OTHER FUN MUST DO ACTIVITES

Ziplining Tour on Keana Farm

If your feeling adventures head on over to Keana Farm where you will have a thrilling time ziplining. The ziplining tour last about 3 hours, where you will be riding in an ATV up to the top. There are 8 different ziplines, with the longest one being half a mine long! Hang tight on the ATV as it will be a bumpy ride to the top! The tour will run you out of pocket around $169 per person.

1 Hour Scenic Horseback Riding at Gunstock Ranch

Looking to unwind from the hustle and bustle? Enjoy a relaxing horseback riding at Gunstock Ranch, where you will be guided through mountain trails, and of course a beautiful scenic view of the ocean. A scene from Hawaii 5-0 was even filmed here! The price per person will set you back by $82, but if you shop around through a booking site, you maybe able to get that price lower.

Parasailing on Waikiki

Enjoy a boat ride on the oceans of Waikiki, where you will be prepared to take off and fly like a bird! Be prepared to get wet, because you will be dipped into the ocean on your way back down! The cost runs around $50-80 per person.

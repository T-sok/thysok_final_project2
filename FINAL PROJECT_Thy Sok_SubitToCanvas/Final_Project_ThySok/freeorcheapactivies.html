FUN ACTIVITES THAT COST YOU NOTHING OR NEXT TO NOTHING

PillBox Hike

The Pillbox Hike is located in Kallia, HI hidden in a neighborhood near Lanikai beach. It's not offically on mapquest so finding the place will be challenging. You'll most likely have to navigate through the neighborhood a few times or stop and ask someone as it is always super busy in the area. There is also no official parking, only side street parking. I've heard hiking to the top to watch the sunrise is the best time but Ive never made it before the sunrise. Nonetheless it doesn't matter what time of the day you go the breath taking view from the top will still be waiting there for you! After your hike walk down to Lanika Beach and take a dip in the warm ocean!

Diamond Head State Monument Hike

Another wonderful hike with breath taking costal views of Waikiki. This hike you'll enjoy more greenery throughout the hike until you reach the top. Be prepared for stairs and going through a narrow tunnel. Ample parking with cheap entrance fee. There is a food truck situated in the parking if you are hungry on your way back down.

Dole Plantation Pineapple Express Train Tour

Situated towards the tail end of the North Shore on the Island of Oahu is the Dole Plantion. There are many things to see, eat and enjoy here! One of them is taking a ride on the Pineapple Express Train. Sit back and enjoy the train ride while listening to the history of Hawaiis Pineapple story.Tickets are $11.50 for adults and $9.50 for children ages 4-12.

DRIVE ALONG THE NORTHSHORE AND STOP BY ALL THE BEACHES OAHU HAS TO OFFER

The best one of all is just being a beach bum and enhaling the smell of the fresh ocean breeze while dipping your toes in the warm ocean water and taking in the beautiful scenery. There are so many beaches along the North Shore and each one of them has something different to offer. Some of my favorites are China Man Hat, Sharks Cove, Sunset Beach and the list goes on! If your an early bird, make sure to catch the surise.
